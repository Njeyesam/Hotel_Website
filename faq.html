Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur extra charges.
Do you offer free Wi-Fi?
Yes, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel for all guests.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
Breakfast is included for selected room packages. Please check your booking details or contact us for confirmation.
Do you have airport shuttle service?
Yes, we offer convenient airport shuttle service. Please request this service when booking or contact our front desk.
Are pets allowed?
Pets are not permitted in the hotel, except for service animals with prior notification.
Is there parking available?
Yes, we provide free secure parking for all hotel guests.
Do you have conference or event facilities?
Yes, we have modern conference rooms and event spaces for meetings, weddings, and other occasions.
How can I modify or cancel my reservation?
You can modify or cancel your reservation by contacting us directly at info@guruinnovationhotel.com or by phone.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers.
Is room service available?
Yes, 24-hour room service is available for food and beverages.
